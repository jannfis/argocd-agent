# argocd-agent's roadmap

The following is a list of things and items we want to support in the future. Here, the future may mean short term, mid term and long term. We have not yet decided on timelines for the items.

## General

* Docs, docs, docs...
* SPIFFE/SPIRE authentication method to support mutual, zero-trust authentication between the control plane and the agents

## Usability

* Provide a CLI to automate bootstrap an agent and its registration with the control plane
